Theory of Computation Studio

PDA Simulator + CFG Simplifier

Work with two independent tools in one place: simulate a single-stack PDA step by step, or enter a context-free grammar and simplify it by removing useless symbols, epsilon-productions, and unit productions.

Workflow 1

Define the PDA 7-tuple

Use `e` or `ε` for epsilon

Transition format: currentState, inputSymbol, stackTop -> nextState, pushSymbols

Examples:

q0, (, Z -> q0, X Z
q0, ), X -> q0, ε
q0, ε, Z -> q_accept, Z

Use comma-separated sets for states and alphabets. Push symbols may be written with spaces (X Z) or, when every stack symbol is one character, as XZ.
